commutePollute setup instructions

  1. register for a new account or login
  2. once you are logged in goto the sensor page of the website or click here
  3. click on "add new sensor"
  4. connect to the hotspot generated by the node that is called commutePollute and has the password commutePollute
  5. navigate to the configuration page when prompted by your operating system or click here when connected to the hotspot, this should then bring you to the configuration page
  6. On the configuration page there should be 2 boxes, one called EUI and the other called appKey.
    • Copy the contents of both into the corresponding boxes on the device configuration page
  7. enter your wifi details and add a password, click save at the bottom of the configuration page, and then reset the node
